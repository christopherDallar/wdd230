Overview

Site Name

" Barquisimeto Chamber of Commerce "

Site Purpose

  • Share with as many people as possible information about the best Businesses to visit in the city of Barquisimeto.
  • Provide a digital place to post new and recent events and a contact list of each member of the chamber of commerce.
  • Encourage people to try to buy what they need from the company affiliated with this commerce chamber or be part of this camera using the page to submit your entry application.

Scenarios

  • How can my company become a member of the chamber?
  • What strategies will the chamber do to promote business to business networking?
  • Which is the inflacion current rate?
  • Which are the recent laws that the government added?
  • Which are the contact information of the members of the chamber?
  • Which are the discounts available for buy products or services in companies affiliated with the chamber?
